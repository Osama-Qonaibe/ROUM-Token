# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that participation in the ROUM Token community will be a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- Age
- Body size
- Disability
- Ethnicity
- Gender identity and expression
- Level of experience
- Nationality
- Personal appearance
- Race
- Religion
- Sexual identity and orientation

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to creating a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Valuing transparency and honesty

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Spreading misinformation or false claims
- Attempting to manipulate or scam community members
- Violating intellectual property rights

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

### Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Report directly:** Email Osamaqonaibe@outlook.com with details
2. **Include context:** Provide specific examples and any supporting links
3. **Confidentiality:** Your report will be handled confidentially

### Consequences

Unacceptable behavior may result in:

- Warning or temporary restriction
- Permanent ban from the community
- Legal action if applicable

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all ROUM Token project spaces including:

- GitHub repository
- Issue tracker
- Pull requests
- Code reviews
- Documentation
- Community communications
- Official events

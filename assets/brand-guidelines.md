### Typography

#### Headings
- **Font Family:** Inter, SF Pro Display, or similar modern sans-serif
- **Weight:** Bold (700) for main headings
- **Weight:** SemiBold (600) for subheadings

#### Body Text
- **Font Family:** Inter, SF Pro Text, or similar
- **Weight:** Regular (400)
- **Weight:** Medium (500) for emphasis

#### Code/Technical
- **Font Family:** Fira Code, JetBrains Mono, or monospace
- **Weight:** Regular (400)
